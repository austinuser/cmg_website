About

Core Values

CMG is a non-profit, student-run management consulting organization at UC Berkeley that provides consulting services for companies in the Bay Area and beyond. We seek to give new perspectives to businesses while providing the experiences for our student consultants to grow professionally. We pride ourselves on delivering creative & implementable solutions developed from comprehensive research efforts.

Commitment to Excellence

We consistently drive our project managers and analysts to think of ways to exceed expectations. We demand more of ourselves than our clients do.

Unconditional Support

We want to be more than an organization, we want to be a support network members can depend on.

Professional Development

We make a tangible effort to develop each member’s professional skills at any point along their career path.

Long-Term Investment

We seek to create a community that extends beyond our members’ college experience resulting in continued mentorship from our newly graduated professionals.

Work

Service Offerings

The CMG approach: CMG understand that the client is always our first priority. Our team members are selectively handpicked and trained with the expertise surrounding your specific project. CMG dives directly into our client’s business challenges and believes in making a true difference in all our projects.

CMG undertakes 3 projects each semester, varying from 10-12 weeks each. Each project consists of two project managers along with four to five analysts, one senior analyst and two project advisors

Current

Why Sponsor

Thank you for your interest in partnering with CMG Strategy Consulting. CMG strives to create a win-win situation and maintain long term relationships with its partners. Your company will have an outlet to connect with the Berkeley community, and leveraging CMG’s established campus connections and commitment to excellence will provide your company a unique competitive advantage.

Our sponsorship benefits include:

  • Exclusive promotion efforts via UC Berkeley exclusive Facebook groups and official website and marketing efforts spearheaded by CMG members
  • Company logo and link on CMG official website and all promotional materials
  • Exclusive access to CMG’s Resume Book
  • Scheduling of internal and external events (case competitions, info-sessions, workshops)
  • Opportunity for company presentation at CMG meetings
